We're seeking multiple Part-time Research Assistants to support our interdisciplinary research projects addressing critical challenges in population health.

## About the Role

As a Research Assistant at GPHA, you'll contribute to:

- Conducting literature reviews and systematic evidence synthesis
- Performing quantitative data analysis using demographic and health datasets
- Supporting computational modeling of population health dynamics
- Preparing research briefs, reports, and policy documents
- Assisting with manuscript preparation for peer-reviewed publications

## Requirements

- Currently enrolled in or recently completed graduate studies (Master's or PhD)
- Strong quantitative and analytical skills
- Proficiency in statistical software (R, Stata, Python, or similar)
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills in English
- Ability to work independently and manage multiple tasks
- Commitment of 10 hours per week

## Nice to Have

We particularly welcome candidates with expertise in one or more of the following areas:

- Computational Social Science: Experience with big data analytics, machine learning applications in social research, network analysis, or agent-based modeling
- Social Epidemiology: Understanding of social determinants of health, health disparities, disease modeling, or population health metrics
- Demography: Skills in population projections, life table analysis, migration studies, or fertility/mortality research
- Health Economics: Knowledge of health system financing, economic evaluation, cost-effectiveness analysis, or health policy modeling

## What We Offer

- Flexible working arrangements (remote or hybrid)
- Opportunity to contribute to high-impact global health research
- Mentorship from leading researchers in population health
- Co-authorship opportunities on publications
- Access to professional development workshops and seminars
- Networking opportunities with international research community
- Letter of recommendation for exceptional performers

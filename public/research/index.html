Our Research Areas

We combine demographic science, computational methods, public health, and policy analysis to understand social change and design interventions that improve lives.

Population Dynamics and Changes

We study fertility, mortality, aging, migration, and urbanization to understand how demographic shifts reshape societies, economies, and environments across regions.

  • Fertility
  • Mortality
  • Migration
  • Demographic modeling and projections
Learn More

AI and Social Transformation

We examine how AI reshapes work, governance, and daily life, and develop responsible, human-centered AI that advances inclusion, transparency, and social good.

  • AI impacts on labor and inequality
  • Algorithmic fairness and governance
  • Data science for social insights
Learn More

Health and Wellbeing Disparities

We analyze the social determinants of health and design strategies to reduce disparities across populations, geographies, and the life course.

  • Social determinants and life-course health
  • Mental health and chronic disease
  • Environmental and place-based health
Learn More

Social Policy and Interventions

We evaluate policies and co-create interventions with communities and institutions to improve equity, resilience, and social protection.

  • Policy design and evaluation
  • Governance and institutional capacity
  • Community-based and digital interventions

Communications Assistant

📍 Remote/Hybrid 💼 Part-time
Join GPHA as a Communications Assistant to amplify our impact through strategic communication and digital engagement in global population health

We’re seeking a dynamic Communications Assistant to support our mission of advancing population health through effective storytelling and strategic communication.

About the Role

As a Communications Assistant at GPHA, you’ll contribute to:

  • Creating engaging content for our website, newsletter, and blog
  • Managing and growing our social media presence across multiple platforms
  • Developing visual materials including infographics, presentations, and reports
  • Supporting event communication for webinars, conferences, and workshops
  • Translating complex research findings into accessible content for diverse audiences
  • Maintaining our digital asset library and communication calendars

Requirements

  • Currently enrolled in or recently completed studies in Communications, Journalism, Public Relations, or related field
  • Strong writing and editing skills with ability to adapt tone for different audiences
  • Experience with social media management and content creation
  • Ability to work independently and meet deadlines
  • Commitment of 5-10 hours per week

Nice to Have

We particularly welcome candidates with:

  • Interest or background in public health, global development, or social sciences
  • Experience with email marketing platforms (Mailchimp, ConvertKit, or similar)
  • Video editing and multimedia production skills
  • Website content management experience (WordPress, Hugo, or similar)
  • Multilingual capabilities, especially in UN languages
  • Experience with academic or non-profit communications

What We Offer

  • Flexible working arrangements (remote or hybrid)
  • Opportunity to communicate impactful global health research to diverse audiences
  • Professional development in science communication and digital marketing
  • Building a portfolio with meaningful global health content
  • Mentorship from experienced communication professionals
  • Networking opportunities with international health and research community
  • Letter of recommendation for exceptional performers

How to Apply

Please send your resume/CV, a brief cover letter to your portfolio demonstrating your communication work.
